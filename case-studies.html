Systems Research & Technical Specs

Rigorous proof-of-concept testing for AI-driven operational scaling.

PoC #01: Autonomous Lead Intelligence

The Challenge: Manual lead qualification was bottlenecking a sales team, costing 15+ hours/week in data entry.

The Logic: Engineered an n8n workflow that scrapes inbound signals, qualifies them via GPT-4o, and routes "hot" leads to Slack instantly.

Technical Stack: n8n, OpenAI API, Custom Python Scrapers, Slack Webhooks.

PoC #02: Content Multiplier Engine

The Challenge: High-ticket consultants wasting 10 hours/week formatting newsletters from video transcripts.

The Logic: A multi-branch system that ingests YouTube URLs, extracts transcripts, and generates SEO-optimized articles and 10+ social snippets.

Technical Stack: Zapier, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Google Docs API, Ghost CMS.
