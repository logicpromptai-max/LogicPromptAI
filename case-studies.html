PoC #01: AI Lead Intelligence Engine

Validation & Testing: 50–100 simulated leads/day; handles malformed inputs; manual QA on accuracy.

Why This Architecture: Event-driven reduces latency; GPT qualification allows flexible criteria; Slack routing minimizes response time.

Scalability Notes: Handles 10× lead volume; modular nodes allow CRM/channel swaps without rebuild.

Visual Logic Flow:

Input → Normalize → Classify → Route → Log
- Lead Intake
- AI Qualification
- Slack Routing
- Notion Logging

Benefit: Saves 20–30 hours/week; reduces missed opportunities.

Scalability Notes: Processes 5× current content volume; modular to plug in new AI models.

Visual Logic Flow:

Submission → Normalize → Classify → Tag → Publish Queue
- Content Intake
- GPT Classification
- Multi-channel Routing
