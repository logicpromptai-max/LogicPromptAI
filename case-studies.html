Systems Research & Technical Specs

These proof-of-concept (PoC) systems demonstrate how LogicPrompt AI validates automation logic. Implementations are tailored per client based on operational context.

PoC #01: Autonomous Lead Intelligence Engine

The Challenge: Manual lead qualification bottlenecks sales growth, costing 15+ hours/week in data entry.

The Logic: n8n workflow scrapes inbound signals, qualifies via GPT-4o, and routes "hot" leads to Slack instantly.

Why This Architecture

  • Event-driven workflows reduce latency vs batch processing.
  • GPT-based qualification allows flexible criteria without constant rule updates.

Validation & Testing

Tested with simulated production inputs (50-100 leads/day) with error-handling for API timeouts.

Scalability: Designed to handle 10x lead volume with zero workflow changes.

PoC #02: Content Multiplier Engine

The Challenge: High-ticket consultants losing 10+ hours/week formatting newsletters from video transcripts.

The Logic: Multi-branch system that extracts YouTube transcripts and generates 10+ SEO-optimized social snippets.

Why This Architecture

  • Modular components allow CRM or channel swaps without rebuilds.
  • Anthropic Claude 3.5 Sonnet utilized for superior long-form creative formatting.

Validation & Testing

Manual QA checks on output accuracy and cross-platform formatting logic.

PoC #03: Ops Triage & Task Orchestration

The Challenge: Operations teams lose visibility when tasks arrive from multiple channels (Email, Slack, Forms).

The Logic: Central intake system normalizes requests, assigns priority via AI classification, and routes to Linear/Notion.

Why This Architecture

  • SLA tagging ensures high-priority requests never breach response windows.
  • Centralized intake prevents "Shadow Ops" and fragmented communication.

Validation & Testing

Stress-tested against malformed inputs and duplicate submission logic.
