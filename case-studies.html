Systems Research & Technical Specs

Proof-of-concept logic validated for real-world operations. Each system is stress-tested before deployment.

PoC #01: AI Lead Intelligence Engine

Validation & Testing: 50–100 simulated leads/day, malformed input handling, QA checked.

Why This Architecture: Event-driven workflow reduces latency, GPT-based qualification, Slack/Gmail routing.

Scalability Notes: Handles 10× lead volume, modular CRM/channel swaps.

Logic Flow:

Input → Normalize → Classify → Route → Log
- Lead Intake
- AI Qualification
- Slack Routing
- Notion Logging

Benefit: Saves 20–30 hours/week, reduces missed opportunities.

PoC #02: Content Automation Engine

Validation & Testing: 25–50 submissions/day, QA on tagging & categorization.

Why This Architecture: GPT-based classification, multi-channel n8n automation, scalable for new AI models.

Scalability Notes: Handles 5× content volume.

Logic Flow:

Submission → Normalize → Classify → Tag → Publish Queue
- Content Intake
- GPT Classification
- Multi-channel Routing

Benefit: Cuts 8–12 hours/week, ensures consistent content quality.

PoC #03: Ops Triage & Task Orchestration

Validation & Testing: Multi-channel task influx, auto SLA tagging, QA checked routing.

Why This Architecture: Central intake reduces lost tasks, AI classification speeds decision-making, modular integration with Notion/Linear/ClickUp.

Scalability Notes: Handles 10× task volume.

Logic Flow:

Request → Normalize → AI Classification → Route → Log
- Intake Normalization
- Priority Classification
- Routing
- SLA Tracker

Benefit: Eliminates missed follow-ups, reduces friction & human error.
